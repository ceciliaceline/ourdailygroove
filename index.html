How it works

  1. ODG (Our Daily Groove) is an open online platform that allows you to express your feelings and thoughts.
  2. In this site, you can send us your thoughts, messages, quotes, or simply anything that you want to share.
  3. Please do include the original sources if the content you’re submitting to us isn’t yours.
  4. An Instagram post will be made based on the message you send.
  5. Messages will be kept confidential and senders can be anonymous if preferred.
  6. If you want to include any name, it’ll be posted along with the message. Or even tag your Instagram account!
  7. If you want to send your message to someone, just give us their Instagram username and we'll tag them in our post!
